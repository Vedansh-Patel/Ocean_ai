# Product Specifications & Business Rules

## Discount Codes
- The discount code **SAVE15** applies a **15% discount** to the total price.
- The discount code **WELCOME10** applies a **$10 flat discount**.
- Invalid codes should show an error message: "Invalid Code".

## Shipping
- **Standard Shipping** is free (0$).
- **Express Shipping** costs $10 added to the total.

## Form Validation
- The **Email** field must contain an "@" symbol. If missing, display "Invalid email" in red.
- The **Name** field is mandatory.

## UI/UX
- The "Pay Now" button must be green.
- Upon successful submission, hide the form and show "Payment Successful!".
